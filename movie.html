TERMINATOR 2
JUDGMENT DAY

TERMINATOR 2 JUDGMENT DAY is a science fiction film released in 1991. Sequel to the first 1984 film “The Terminator”. The T-1000 Terminator was sent from the future by Skynet to kill John Connor who will grow up and lead the Human Resistance against Skynet. The T-800 Terminator was sent back to protect John Connor from the T-1000. While the T-1000 and John Connor form a friendship.

Starring Arnold Schwarzenegger as the Terminator, Robert Partrick as the T-1000, Edward Furlong as John Connor and Linda Hamilton as Sarah Connor.

While 2 judgment day is quite an unrealistic film, it does have some realistic moments in the movie. The nuke scene at the end of the film was the best example of a nuke. It is known as one of the most realistic descriptions of a nuclear holocaust. Also, T-800 forming a human connection with John Connor seems realistic because of the new Ai.
